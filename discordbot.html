Veyah

Veyah is a discord bot that I'm making cause I'm awesome sauce. designed for me and other people.

Features

  • Mini-games: Hangman, Wordle, Rock Paper Scissors, Connect 4
  • XP System: Levels, leaderboards, and rank-up messages
  • Birthday & To-Do System: Track birthdays and personal tasks
  • Fun commands: Truth, Dare, Would You Rather, 8ball
  • Utility: serverinfo, userinfo, poll, uptime, avatar, date, and more
